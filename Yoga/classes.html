Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the picture of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on Asana (proper alignment and posture), Pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although deisgned for intermediate to advanced student, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movemnent-you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restrative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long posees that are supported by a chair or wall. this calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level with this experience. This practice can be a perfect way to rehabiliate an injury.
